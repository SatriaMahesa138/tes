Tegalalang Rice Terrace

Here we can enjoy beautiful & beautiful views of the rice fields. There is also a swing that allows us to enjoy beautiful and stunning views from a height, and also a flying fox that can be enjoyed.

Swing

Here we can play on the swings, while enjoying the green rice fields. What makes it even more enjoyable is the beautiful air.

Monkey Forest

Monkey Forest is one of the places frequently visited by foreign tourists. because we can interact directly with the monkeys, apart from that, the forest is still very natural as a gathering place for monkeys, which also adds to the attraction.

Ubud Palace

Ubud Palace is a historical building complex, the official residence of the Ubud royal family which has been made into a tourist attraction
