Beratan Lake

is a lake located in the Bedugul area. In the middle of the lake there is a temple, namely Pura Ulun Danu, which is a place of worship to Sang Hyang Dewi Danu as the giver of fertility.

Banyumala Waterfall

Buleleng Regency has several beautiful waterfalls and is popular among tourists. The air around Banyumala Waterfall is very cool, because it is located at an altitude. Around the road to the waterfall, it is filled with local gardens, adding to the beauty of this place.

Twin Lake

Lake Buyan and Lake Tamblingan are like triplet lakes located close to each other and can be easily accessed from Buleleng city center, approximately an hour's drive. Lake Buyan is one of the largest lakes in Bali which is located next to Lake Beratan and Lake Tamblingan. These three lakes have an interrelated history.
