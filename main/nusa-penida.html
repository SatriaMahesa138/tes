Kelingking Beach

It is located on a cliff with an elongated hill that juts out towards the sea and looks like a little finger. The view of Kelingking Beach from above is the exotic view of Tebing Paluang, or also called Karang Dewa. Kelingking Paluang is the name of a temple which is located not far from this place, while Karang Desa is the name of the local village. This part of the cliff is often a photo spot because of its unique shape. The cliffs create a depression into the beach.

Broken Beach

Iconic beach with views of coral rocks that collapse to form a circle above sea level. As the name suggests, Broken Beach has an imperfect beach view. However, imperfection is what makes this beach interesting. The panorama of this beach is truly amazing. Starting from the blue sea water, surrounded by coral rocks, and one part of the coral forms a silhouette like a tunnel above the sea water.

Angel Billabong

Angel's Billabong has very clear water so it will present beautiful natural views. Its extraordinary clarity allows you to clearly see the seabed which is decorated with colorful coral reefs and the various marine life dancing in it. Angel's Billabong is also an ideal place for nature photography lovers. The clear water creates the perfect view for photographing the natural beauty of the surroundings. The sturdy rock cliffs combined with clear water and blue skies create a very beautiful visual composition.

Cristal Bay

Even though it is called Crystal Bay Nusa Penida, this beach is better known or referred to as Penida Beach. Crystal Bay itself is basically a popular name given by foreign tourists. Crystal Bay Nusa Penida has easy access. You can use a scooter from Toyapakeh Harbor on the new road to get to your destination. Crystal Bay itself is a place that is famous for its dominant scuba diving or snorkeling activities so it is included in the top 5 places to see on Nusa Penida Island
