Telletubbies

Having the attraction of green hills with mounds and beautiful valleys similar to those in the Teletubbies series, makes Teletubbies Hill a popular tourist destination in Nusa Penida. It's not just a vacation spot. Teletubbies Hill Nusa Penida is also often used as a photo spot for pre-wedding, you know. Especially for couples who like natural nuances, this hill is even more attractive and instagrammable.

Rumah Pohon

located in Banjar Pelilit, Pejukutan Village, Nusa Penida, Klungkung, Bali. Travelers will be spoiled by the amazing view of Atuh Beach from a height. The panorama of small islands, towering cliffs and rows of hills will make the traveler amazed. Travelers don't need to worry about having to climb trees to reach the top of the tree house, because it is equipped with stairs. Travelers can have a picnic at the Batu Molenteng Tree House, so don't forget to prepare food and other equipment.

Diamond Beach

A hidden paradise in Bali located on Nusa Penida Island. This beach is famous for the beauty of its fine white sand, clear sea water and towering coral cliffs around it. It gets its name from its jewel-like shape, with white sand that shines in the sun.

Paluang Beach

is a tourist spot that has many interesting photo spots. From here, visitors can enjoy views of Kelingking Beach from a height with an area resembling a boat on a cliff. Many visitors come to hunt for aesthetic photos at this favorite spot. So on weekends the location is quite busy and requires visitors to queue.
