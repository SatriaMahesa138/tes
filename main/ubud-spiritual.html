Tirta Empul Temple

is a Hindu temple in the center of the island of Bali, Indonesia, precisely in Manukaya Village, Tampaksiring District, Gianyar Regency, which is famous for its holy water where Balinese Hindus seek purification.

Goa Gajah Temple

Gajah Cave is an artificial cave from ancient times that functions as a place of worship. This cave is located in Bedulu Village, Blahbatuh District, Gianyar Regency, Bali.
